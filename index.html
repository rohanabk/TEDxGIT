    About

    TED

    TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages. Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world.


    TEDx, x = independently organised TED event

    A TEDx event is a local gathering where live TED-like talks and performances are shared with the community. TEDx events are fully planned and coordinated independently, on a community-by-community basis. The content and design of each TEDx event is unique and developed independently, but all of them have many features in common.


    TEDxKLSGIT

    TEDxKLSGIT is an independently run, one of a kind event in the vicinity of the city of Belgaum and is a stepping stone to tell the people about innovation, inspiration and intellect in the fields of technology, entertainment and design converged into a single mesmerizing event for those who seek diversity and experience. TEDxKLSGIT is coming to town for the second time this year with a commitment towards making a superceding impact in the minds of those who seek knowledge and wisdom. This year TEDxKLSGIT comes to the crowds with a trailblazing show of ideas to ASTOUND, ENTHRALL AND INSPIRE the masses!
